📜 Class Rules

Everything you need to know before joining.

🏛️ Class Structure

  • 1 Term = 1 Hour of class
  • Students attend 2–3 classes per week
  • Batch timings assigned after registration
  • Students grouped by age and skill level

📅 Attendance Policy

  • Missed classes can be adjusted within the plan period
  • Classes cannot be carried forward after plan expires
  • Please inform 1 hour before a missed class
  • Make-up classes subject to availability

🎨 Art Supplies

  • Basic supplies provided during class
  • Students may bring their own materials
  • Recommended supply list shared on enrollment

💳 Payment Policy

  • Payment due before plan start date
  • Plans non-refundable once started
  • WhatsApp Meghna for payment confirmation
